Boxing

The Vault Gym has its own dedicated boxing room. It’s supremely well equipped and has seen world champion fighters such as David Haye and Amir Khan train in the space.

Unlike many health clubs that offer boxing classes we have professional boxing coaches sanctioned by the British Boxing Board of Control.

We also have strong links to white-collar boxing organisations, should you want to face the ultimate challenge of going toe to toe in the ring.
