Shoreditch Gym

This is The Vault - 5,000 sq ft of premium gym space located in the heart of Shoreditch. We’re fully equipped with free weights, cable and cardio machines, and multiple areas including a heavy weights room, a boxing room and a yoga studio to facilitate functional training.

The Vault is unique because everyone who trains here works with their own PT on a 1:1 basis which means we can offer a very private, premium environment to work out. No one ever has to wait for machines, or fight for weights - instead, time in the gym is fully maximized.

The gym was designed to complement the integrity of the original building, and we collaborated with artists, bespoke furniture makers and lighting specialists to create a space that is not only functional, but incredibly cool and unique.

Naturally, we’re fully air-conditioned, but because no one wants to work out in stale second-hand air, The Vault is fitted with a fresh air ventilation system which pumps lovely fresh outside air all around the gym.
