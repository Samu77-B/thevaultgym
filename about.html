About

The Vault Gym was founded by Jon and Suzy, who turned their shared love of sport into a lifelong mission. Married in 2003, they established The Vault in 2010 after years of involvement in competitive sport and a growing belief that the fitness industry should be more than a conveyor belt of generic classes.

Jon has been involved in sport and athletics all his life, and it was his national achievements on the track as a 400m hurdler which led him to study exercise science at an academic level. A qualified PT of over 30 years, Jon trains his own private clients in the gym.

Suzy is horse mad, and as well as being a showjumper, she also used to compete in various showing events. She’s now a qualified PT and yoga teacher specialising in Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin and Power Yoga, and her little oasis in central Shoreditch is the yoga room at The Vault.

Their vision was to create a training environment that supports exceptional fitness professionals and gives them the space to do what they do best. The Vault brings together ambitious, highly skilled PTs with people who want highly personalised 1:1 training sessions.

Jon and Suzy believe that training should be something people look forward to. At The Vault every detail of the experience matters. That’s why the space is designed to be stylish, comfortable, and genuinely welcoming - a place where great coaching and a strong community come together.
