Nutrition

The key to success in any exercise program is nutrition, and at The Vault Gym we offer a wide range of nutrition programs to help you to succeed.

All of our trainers have excellent nutritional knowledge but if you need more specific advice we have clinicians to help.

We are one of the few gyms to offer blood tests, where we send your blood to be checked in a laboratory for allergies. We can then offer a whole supplement range to resolve any issues.

If you lean more towards organic food nutrition we can also provide eating plans to resolve any issues.

Our main priority is your health and we are not a company that will just fill you full of drugs or supplements. We have a highly educated team here to help you.
